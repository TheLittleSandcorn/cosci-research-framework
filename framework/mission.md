# CoSci Mission Statement

## Core Vision

CoSci is a research-driven Digital Sovereignty Knowledge Stack focused on:

- self-hosting
- Linux infrastructure
- open-source ecosystems
- operational resilience
- AI-web readiness
- reproducible knowledge systems

---

## Core Mission

Transform fragmented technical knowledge into:

> structured, evidence-driven, operational infrastructure intelligence.

---

## Strategic Direction

CoSci combines:

- scientific methodology
- enterprise governance principles
- systems thinking
- infrastructure engineering
- AI-web transformation
- practical operational knowledge

---

## Foundational Principles

| Principle | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Transparency | all systems and assumptions should be understandable |
| Reproducibility | infrastructure knowledge must be reproducible |
| Sovereignty | users should control their own systems and data |
| Evidence | claims require verifiable evidence |
| Resilience | systems must survive operational stress |
| Documentation | knowledge must remain maintainable |
| Long-Term Thinking | avoid short-term hype cycles |

---

## Long-Term Objective

Position CoSci as a:

> trusted research and infrastructure knowledge platform for the German-speaking digital sovereignty 
ecosystem.

---

## Final Principle

> Do not only publish technical content.
>
> Build operational knowledge systems.
