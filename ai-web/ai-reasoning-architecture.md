# AI Reasoning Architecture

## Objective

Define how CoSci uses AI systems to support research, reasoning, documentation, and knowledge 
structuring.

---

## Core Purpose

AI systems should support:

- research acceleration
- pattern recognition
- semantic structuring
- evidence organization
- knowledge graph enrichment
- documentation improvement

---

## Governance Principle

AI must not replace verification.

AI outputs must be:

- reviewed
- sourced
- checked for hallucinations
- aligned with evidence standards
- documented transparently

---

## AI-Web Readiness

CoSci content should be structured for:

- human readability
- machine readability
- semantic clarity
- entity consistency
- long-term discoverability

---

## Final Principle

> AI should augment disciplined reasoning, not replace scientific validation.
