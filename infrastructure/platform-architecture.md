# CoSci Platform Architecture

## Objective

Design a sovereign research and intelligence infrastructure stack for scalable scientific and 
operational knowledge systems.

---

## Core Infrastructure Layers

### 1. Research Layer

Purpose:
- collect
- validate
- structure
- compare

research intelligence.

---

### 2. Knowledge Graph Layer

Purpose:
- connect entities
- map relationships
- generate operational context
- support AI reasoning systems.

---

### 3. Governance Layer

Purpose:
- ensure traceability
- maintain scientific standards
- establish auditability
- define operational ethics.

---

### 4. Infrastructure Layer

Purpose:
- scalable deployment
- sovereign hosting
- distributed systems
- secure data operations.

---

### 5. AI Systems Layer

Purpose:
- semantic analysis
- pattern recognition
- research automation
- intelligence augmentation.

---

## Long-Term Goal

Build a European sovereign operational intelligence ecosystem.
